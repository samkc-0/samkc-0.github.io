Neuroplasticity & Growth Mindset

8 May 2021

Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity refers our brain's ability to prune and form new connections between neurons based on our experiences. It doesn't end in childhood, but rather continues throughout our lifespan, which implies you can continue to pick up new skills at any age based on where you focus your attention.

Growth Mindset

Growth mindset refers to a mindset you can have where you believe you can continue to grow as a person so long as you actually do something about it. The term is used in contrast to a fixed mindset where one is preoccupied with "innate" talents or things you learnt as a child and believes it's too late or otherwise not within your capabilities to change.
