the DOM

Hey, non-technical friend, the DOM is like a big bag of functions that let you grab things on the web-page and change them. That's what makes web pages interactive. For example when you go to those webpages that have a red div and you click the button that says "click me" and the div changes to green, that's all thanks to the DOM.
