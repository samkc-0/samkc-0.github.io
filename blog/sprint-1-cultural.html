Values

20 April 2021

I had to make an ethical decision once when my phone broke and I wanted to get a new one. I wanted a phone that was ethically produced, so I looked around for brands that were ethically produced, but they didn’t ship to New Zealand, so I just got an unethically produced phone. Consequences… never mind.

Due to my culture, I identify as a ‘Christmas celebrator’, and value spending time with family and visiting crowded malls at least once a year. Actually the family thing turns out not to be cultural.

My strengths & limitations can be found in my learning plan.

Once I was trying to work productively but my cat kept walking across the desk, so I walked outside and then he walked outside, and then I ran back inside and closed the door until I was finished working. We're still friends, and I'd do it again.
