EQ

23 April 2021

Emotional Intelligence is supposedly a set of skills that make you a good leader or people-person and also the title of a famous book by journalist Daniel Goleman. IQ is a score you get from doing a test, and the score is meant to correlate with other things like income or academic performance. The basic idea of this unit seems to be that being an alright person can have as many advantages (or more than) being good at more abstract cognitive challenges.
